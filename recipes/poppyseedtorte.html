Lemon Poppy Seed Torte

This is a traditional Ukrainian torte, made even better with a tart rasberry curd filling, and a white chocolate whipped cream frosting. This torte is a tad bit heavier than poppy seed bakes you usually see, and the honey in the recipe gives it a distinct flavor and a richer texture. Now that I've introduced you to this Ukrainian delight, let's get stuck in!

Ingredients

For Torte

  • 1 cup poppy seeds
  • 1 cup white granulated sugar
  • 1 cup honey (melted, let cool)
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup coffee, cooled
  • 6 eggs, beaten until thick
  • 3 cups all purpose flour (unbleached)
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Dash of salt

Rasberry Curd Filling

  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/2 pint fresh raspberries (1/2 of 12 oz. package frozen raspberries, thawed)
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 1/3 cup white granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp fresh lemon juice

Garnish

  • Fresh raspberries
  • Lemon peelings

White Chocolate Whipped Frosting

  • 4 cups whipping cream (33-35% fat)
  • 2 bars of white baking chocolate (white chips WILL not work)
